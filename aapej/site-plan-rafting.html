Overview

Purpose

Three river rafting is the biggest, not to mention the only company in the city of Jeremie that is involved in rafting, as it is a great and interesting sport in the country. Our goal is to allow all swimmers of responsible age to practice this exciting sport through the quality services we offer...

Audience

What is the importance of participating? Here are three big reasons:

  1. Meeting new people: Rafting is a team sport and thus a great way to meet new people! Teams are made out of 6-8 people who share the experience together. Who knows, maybe you meet your future partner on a rafting trip…
  2. Bonding: Another social aspect of rafting is definitely creating stronger relationship bonds through the experience of challenging rapids with teamwork and enjoying the scenery together…
  3. Refreshment on hot summer days: On hot summer days you can’t imagine better refreshment then the cold and clean water from the river. Swim in it, jump in to it, splash other rafters or just enjoy being splashed from other people. It is your choice!
