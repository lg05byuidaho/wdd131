History

Learn about our journey, achievements, and the milestones we have reached.

It is found between the socio-cultural and educational animators adhered to this status, a non-profit association, in accordance with the provisions of article 31 of the Haitian Constitution of 1987 amended, the laws of the Republic and the International Conventions applicable to it.

Our Goals

Promote popular education as a development tool through animation for the benefit of youth.

Specific Objectives

  • Bring together animators around an idea and a common interest
  • Ensure the education and training of young people from all backgrounds in order to promote living together
  • Organize socio-cultural, educational and sports activities for the development of youth
  • Supervise children and young people.
  • Support the field of socio-cultural, educational and sports activities

The values that bring us together:

  • Living together
  • Gender equity
  • Civic-mindedness
  • Secularism
